Methodology Summary

This summary is intentionally concise. The buyer does not need the full internal engine, but they do need a legible explanation of what the score means and how the recommendation was formed.

Benchmark Logic

What `Norynthe.Score` means

`Norynthe.Score` is a benchmark-composite trust score computed from Norynthe’s controlled question bank rather than arbitrary prompts or isolated interactions.

  • 15 benchmark questions
  • 6 public score dimensions + 1 diagnostic comparative signal
  • The same evaluation surface across all compared models
Review Logic

How scores are formed

Each benchmark result is evaluated dimension by dimension, challenged through a scorer / critic structure, and then resolved into a final benchmark-composite score and recommendation layer.

  • Question-level benchmarking
  • Dimension-level trust analysis
  • Evidence and critique behind the final result
